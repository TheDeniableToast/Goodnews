About GoodNews.com

GoodNews.com is an online newssite with the intent to bring you the greatest news that the internet has to offer.

Here you can find the latest positive news from around the world. Whether you are looking for good local news or good world news, GoodNews.com is the place to find it

GoodNews.com was created as a contrast to the largely negative news sources that we have. Because everyone needs some good news.

We find it important to look on the bright side every once in a while and this website is made to help you with that.

If there is something that you want to show or tell us, such as problems or technical difficulties, please contact us through our Email. support@goodnews.com
